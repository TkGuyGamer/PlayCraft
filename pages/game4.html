Game:

This is Bullet Box 1.0. A game where you have to survive incoming bullets and falling boxes. You main aim is to collect boxes and shoot them to their rightful landing pads. Beware though if you have no boxes in you inventory your health bar decreases and if you do have boxes your health decreases but at a faster pace. So the health bar is unstable thus this game was made for the 2025 Bullet hell
