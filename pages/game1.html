Game:

North Pole Delivers is a fun and engaging game based on the theme of Christmas and gift rumble. It includes 1 additional mini game. So your main goal is to deliver as many presents as you can and have fun. You are a Santa who is just doing his job, but you are attacked by incoming rockets from naughty kids who are angry at you so beware.
